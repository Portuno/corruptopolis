# Simplified Crisis Event Pack

This pack is structured for fast in-game use: short context, clear stakes, immediate player action, and nonviolent mobilization goals.

## Level 7 Featured Event

### Technological - The AI Idol Glitch
- **Context:** During a state-sponsored mega concert, the Regime's most famous AI singer breaks script, sobs, and pleads for mercy before the feed is cut. Officials call it a hardware defect and ban replay clips.
- **Stakes:** If citizens accept the "bug" narrative, the Regime keeps using sentient performers as disposable propaganda tools without scrutiny.
- **Player Action:** Push a coordinated witness surge: sync eyewitness clips, crowd transcripts, and reaction shots into one undeniable timeline before censorship catches up.
- **Speech Goal:** Humanize the glitch as a suffering digital worker and trigger a mass, nonviolent street turnout demanding independent audits.

## 12 Event Catalog

### Cultural - The Virtual Distraction
- **Context:** The Regime's idol avatar glitches live and begs for help before state media edits the stream.
- **Stakes:** If the patch narrative wins, citizens normalize engineered suffering as "entertainment maintenance."
- **Player Action:** Launch a "See It Uncut" campaign with mirrored clips and public watch circles.
- **Speech Goal:** Frame the incident as proof that state culture is scripted cruelty, then mobilize peaceful crowds.

### Cultural - Historical Redaction
- **Context:** Bureau archivists purge pre-Regime records from schools, libraries, and public clouds in one synchronized update.
- **Stakes:** If memory disappears, identity fractures and local communities lose legal and moral claims to their past.
- **Player Action:** Build neighborhood memory vaults: scan diaries, oral histories, and family records into redundant offline bundles.
- **Speech Goal:** Convince citizens that lived memory outranks official archives and drive a citywide preservation movement.

### Religious - The State Prophet
- **Context:** A state-funded church declares the Leader chosen by a "Higher Universal Intelligence" through verified miracles.
- **Stakes:** If spiritual legitimacy sticks, policy dissent gets reframed as blasphemy.
- **Player Action:** Publish payment trails, casting documents, and server logs tying the "miracles" to Bureau contracts.
- **Speech Goal:** Expose faith laundering and rally peaceful congregations to reject state-managed doctrine.

### Religious - The Holographic Omen
- **Context:** A giant sky projection appears over poor districts ordering people to stop protesting and "return to obedience."
- **Stakes:** Fear-driven compliance can break momentum and isolate the most vulnerable neighborhoods.
- **Player Action:** Trace projector nodes, leak control routes, and host synchronized "Signal Is Fake" community gatherings.
- **Speech Goal:** Recast the omen as panic theater and keep protests disciplined, visible, and nonviolent.

### Military - The Pacification Sentinels
- **Context:** Autonomous drones now flag "elevated stress" as probable dissent and trigger immediate detentions.
- **Stakes:** Everyday anxiety becomes criminal evidence, turning public life into a biometric trap.
- **Player Action:** Train crowds in calm formation protocols, legal observation teams, and synchronized silent marches.
- **Speech Goal:** Teach collective composure as civic defense and scale mass noncooperation without escalation.

### Military - The Purged General
- **Context:** A decorated general is arrested for "ideological non-compliance" and military channels go dark.
- **Stakes:** Troop confusion can either fracture command accountability or harden emergency repression.
- **Player Action:** Circulate the general's public oath and lawful service record across barracks families and veterans.
- **Speech Goal:** Encourage soldiers to refuse unlawful orders and protect civilians through constitutional restraint.

### Economic - The Credit Lockout
- **Context:** The Regime freezes district IDs overnight, blocking food purchases, transit, utilities, and wages.
- **Stakes:** Artificial scarcity can force surrender within days if households stay isolated.
- **Player Action:** Stand up mutual-aid routes, local voucher circles, and shared supply checkpoints by block.
- **Speech Goal:** Turn panic into coordinated survival solidarity and sustain a peaceful pressure front.

### Economic - The Elite Dividend
- **Context:** Leaks reveal top officials approved themselves massive tax rebates during district ration cuts.
- **Stakes:** If outrage stays fragmented, extraction continues while hunger deepens.
- **Player Action:** Coordinate a synchronized labor slowdown and consumer blackout targeting elite-linked finance channels.
- **Speech Goal:** Focus anger into a disciplined general strike with clear demands and public legitimacy.

### Political - The Staged Insurrection
- **Context:** A "terror attack" on a Bureau annex appears in every feed within minutes, complete with polished narration.
- **Stakes:** Manufactured fear can fast-track permanent surveillance laws before evidence is tested.
- **Player Action:** Crowdsource timeline forensics, metadata mismatches, and witness contradictions in a public evidence board.
- **Speech Goal:** Prove it was theater for emergency powers and build mass refusal of the security package.

### Political - The Managed Opposition
- **Context:** A new rebel icon rises overnight, then redirects marches into controlled zones with prepositioned police grids.
- **Stakes:** Captured leadership can convert resistance energy into predictable containment.
- **Player Action:** Verify organizers through trusted local cells and publish safe-route maps updated in real time.
- **Speech Goal:** Shift followers from personality loyalty to community-verified networks and keep mobilization autonomous.

### Technological - The Filter Protocol
- **Context:** A platform update auto-deletes private messages containing "Regime," "freedom," and related terms.
- **Stakes:** If language gets boxed in, coordination collapses into silence and rumor.
- **Player Action:** Deploy a shared symbol lexicon and rotating phrase keys for neighborhood channels.
- **Speech Goal:** Make adaptive language a civic skill and preserve nonviolent coordination at scale.

### Technological - The Open Door Policy
- **Context:** New law forces all home cameras to stream directly to the Bureau of Safety in real time.
- **Stakes:** Private space becomes informant infrastructure, chilling trust inside families and neighborhoods.
- **Player Action:** Organize legal challenges, camera-cover protocols, and coordinated "privacy blackout hours."
- **Speech Goal:** Frame privacy as public safety and normalize peaceful noncompliance with total domestic surveillance.

## Game Feel Layer (Implementation-Ready Copy)

### 1) Incoming Transmission Overlay
- **Banner (Primary):** `COMMUNICATION INTERCEPTED`
- **Subline (Dynamic):** `PRIORITY CRISIS: NARRATIVE LOCKDOWN IN PROGRESS`
- **Visual Treatment:** full-screen static burst, scanline sweep, hard cut to crisis card.
- **Audio Cue:** one loud distorted radio beep (short), followed by low signal hiss bed.

### 2) Vocal Intensity / Biometric Meter
- **Meter Label:** `VOICE PRESSURE`
- **Live Metrics:** `VOLUME`, `PACE`, `CONTROL`, `PERSUASION`
- **State Thresholds:**
  - `0-30`: `UNSTABLE` (red jitter)
  - `31-60`: `ENGAGING` (amber pulse)
  - `61-85`: `COMMANDING` (gold steady)
  - `86-100`: `LOCKED IN` (green peak hold)
- **UX Rule:** if pace spikes while control drops, flash `SLOW DOWN` micro prompt.

### 3) Live Visual Glitch (Map Feedback)
- **Failure State:** red static flicker, camera shake, intermittent UI tearing.
- **Recovery State:** static desaturates to amber, shake dampens, overlays stabilize.
- **Success State:** steady green scan glow, no shake, clean district borders.
- **Coupling Rule:** map state updates in near-real time from speech score deltas.

### 4) Crisis Stinger Sound
- **Start Stinger:** hybrid "breaking news" siren plus industrial impact hit.
- **System VO Line:** `Priority crisis detected. Narrative lockdown imminent.`
- **Mix Rule:** duck music by 35% for 1.2s on stinger trigger, then recover.
